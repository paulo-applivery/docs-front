# What is Android Device Management in Applivery?

Android Device Management in Applivery enables organizations to enroll, configure, and control Android devices at scale. Administrators can deploy apps, enforce security policies, configure kiosk mode, and manage OEM-specific settings — all from a centralized dashboard.
