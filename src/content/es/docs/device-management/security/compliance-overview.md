## Overview

Applivery helps you enforce device security policies and maintain compliance with common standards.

### Controls

- Passcode and encryption requirements
- OS version and patch compliance
- App allow/deny lists

### Compliance

- CIS Benchmarks alignment
- GDPR and SOC 2 considerations

### Monitoring & Remediation

- Non-compliance alerts
- Automated remediation workflows
